Cappuccino

 

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, and is traditionally prepared with double espresso, and steamed milk foam.

Espresso

 

Espresso is coffee brewed by expressing or forcing out a small amount of nearly boiling water under pressure through finely ground coffee beans. Espresso has a higher concentration of suspended and dissolved solids, and has crema on top.

Latte

 

A latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

Americano

 

Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from traditionally brewed coffee. The strength of an Americano varies with the number of shots of espresso and the amount of water added.

Macchiato

 

Macchiato is an espresso coffee drink with a small amount of milk, usually foamed.

Mochaccino

 

A caffè mocha, also called mocaccino, is a chocolate-flavored variant of a caffè latte.
